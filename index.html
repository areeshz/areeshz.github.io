Petsy: E-Commerce

Technologies Used

  • ReactJS
  • React Bootstrap
  • HTML / CSS
  • Stripe Payment Integration
  • Node.js
  • Express.js
  • MongoDB
  • Mongoose

Petsy is an E-Commerce store built in ReactJS where users can purchase supplies and toys for their little furry friends. Users can view all available products, and upon sign-in, can add items to their cart. Users can complete mock-payments with the integrated Stripe payment system, and view their past orders within the app.

Trip Planner

Technologies Used

  • JavaScript
  • jQuery
  • HTML/CSS/Sass
  • Bootstrap
  • HandlebarsJS
  • Node.js
  • Express.js
  • MongoDB
  • Mongoose

Trip Planner is a full-stack single page application that allows users to plan the details of their next adventure or record the highlights of their past trips. Upon sign-in, users have access to all of their logged trips, separated into past trips and future trips. In addition to adding, editing, and deleting trip details, users can also keep a log of events related to each trip.
